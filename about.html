SC2Share.com = simple Starcraft II replays

SC2Share.com is like Twitpic or bit.ly for Starcraft II replays. Simply send a file and we generate a tiny URL that can be shared in forums, emails and Twitter.

Why another replay site?

There are plenty of great Starcraft II replay sites out there. We like them all, and we're not here to replace them.

SC2Share.com is here when you need to quickly upload a Starcraft II replay. There are no accounts, no forums, your replay will not be discussed on this site. It's ok to upload replays that you don't want to show off, but that you still want to share with friends.

Less is more

Our short URLs link to replay files, not pages full of advertisements, download queues and offers of premium services.

And since all we share is replay files, you know that URLs that start with http://sc2share.com/ are safe. We don't accept executable files, images, videos or non-replay, unsafe data.

Suggestions?

If you have suggestions or bug reports, you can mail guilherme@sc2share.com.
